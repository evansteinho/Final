About Us

We craft Asian Dim Sum Specialties, gourmet "fusion" hor d'oeuvres, customized appetizers, and natural wholesome foods... all made with the finest quality ingredients.

Our products are crafted from the finest ingredients, each accompanied by a Letter of Guarantee from our esteemed local, national, and international vendors. As a licensed SQF/USDA/FDA manufacturer and distributor, we uphold the highest standards in gourmet food production, catering to a diverse clientele including hotels, restaurants, retail grocers, and various institutions across the hospitality and transportation industries.
